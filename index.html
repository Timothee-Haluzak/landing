What course should I choose ?

Beginner English Course: The Beginner English Course is designed for customers who have little to no experience with the English language. Customers will learn the basics of English grammar, vocabulary, and pronunciation. They will also learn how to form simple sentences, ask and answer basic questions, and engage in simple conversations. Additionally, customers will learn basic reading and writing skills, such as how to read and write basic English texts.

Intermediate English Course: The Intermediate English Course is designed for customers who have a basic understanding of the English language and want to improve their skills further. Customers will learn more complex grammar structures, a wider range of vocabulary, and how to speak English with greater fluency and accuracy. They will also learn how to express their opinions, share ideas, and engage in more in-depth conversations. Additionally, customers will learn how to read and write more complex English texts.

Advanced English Course: The Advanced English Course is designed for customers who have a strong command of the English language and want to refine their skills further. Customers will learn advanced grammar structures, an extensive range of vocabulary, and how to speak English with a high degree of fluency and accuracy. They will also learn how to express complex ideas, engage in debates, and participate in discussions on a variety of topics. Additionally, customers will learn how to read and write complex and technical English texts, such as academic papers and professional documents.
